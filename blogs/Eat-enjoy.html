Eat Enjoy American Kitchen in Linkou, Taiwan

After a day of shopping at the Linkou Mitsui Outlet, my boyfriend and I decided to have dinner at a nearby restaurant with a high Google rating of 4.6 stars: Eat Enjoy American Kitchen. We were delighted to find out that this restaurant is also part of the Ocard network, offering special treats for members. Needless to say, we quickly signed up!

Our Order

1. Creamy Mentaiko Shrimp Risotto - NT$299 (+NT$40 for baked cheese topping)

The risotto was a perfect blend of creamy mentaiko and fresh shrimp, topped with a generous layer of melted cheese. Each grain of rice was soaked in the rich sauce, creating a delightful, flavorful bite every time.

2. Creamy Garlic Beef Belly Risotto - NT$279 (+NT$40 for baked cheese topping)

This dish featured tender slices of beef belly in a creamy garlic sauce, again topped with baked cheese. The risotto was rich and savory, with every bite bursting with flavor.

3. Crispy Battered Fries - NT$0 (Member gift)

These fries were a delightful member gift. Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, they were perfect for dipping in the leftover crab sauce.

4. Creamy Crab Sauce Bread - NT$0 (Member gift)

Another fantastic member gift, this bread was served with a creamy crab sauce. The fresh crab flavor melded perfectly with the rich white sauce, and we even used the leftover sauce to dip our fries in. It was simply outstanding.

The main courses were both enhanced by the addition of baked cheese, making them even more indulgent.
